Data source: Our World In Data

December 2, 2020: Our World In Data originally obtained their data from ECDC. They have since switched data source to John Hopkins, and the daily numbers looked different from ECDC/RKI, due to different ways of updating their data. Therefore I have switched data source to RKI which seems to be the primary source for ECDC.

Cumulative number of confirmed SARS-CoV-2 cases in Germany in linear scale since March 15, 2020



Number of confirmed new cases in Germany since March 15, 2020

This plot shows the increase in the number of confirmed new cases. Each data point is averaged over 7 previous consecutive days . Note: This is not necessarily reflective in the increase of actual number of cases each day, since the number of tests for the virus has been increasing as the pandemic progressed.

December 28, 2020: Note that during the Christmas holidays the numbers are artificially low as there are fewer tests conducted, fewer people go get tested, and also the reporting of numbers are delayed.
